About Our Founder

Meet Apostle Veronica Hadassah Jones, the visionary leader behind Seven Ministries International School

Veronica Hadassah Jones

Apostle, Prophet and Visionary Leader of the 21st Century

Seven Ministries International is part of Veronica Hadassah Jones Ministries International. The Founder is Apostle Veronica Hadassah Jones.

Apostle Veronica Hadassah Jones is a Powerful, Heavily Anointed Prophet and Visionary Leader of the 21st Century who has many industry activities including a Powerful Apostolic Ministry.

Ministry Impact

She has laid hands on many sick people who received instant healing including people with terminal cancer both in the United Kingdom and in Ghana.

Miraculous Event

Apostle Veronica Hadassah Jones has laid hands on a woman who was dead and she came back to life, the Apostle disappeared quietly whiles a crowd gathered around in amazement as she does not like much attention to herself. This happened in Amarhia, on the Dodowa Road in January 2022.

Business Leadership

Apostle Veronica runs a successful business within the personal care and cosmetics manufacturing industry, she was called into full time Ministry by The Lord Jesus Christ.

Education & Experience

The Apostle had her secondary school education at St. Mary's Girls Secondary School in Accra, her tertiary education includes a masters degree from Kingston University, London following her first degree from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology. She has many years experience working in the National Health Service (NHS) in the United Kingdom. She has also worked on International Quality Assurance schemes for medical Laboratories for both England and the World Health Organisation.

Her Vision for Education

Apostle Veronica's love for young children blessed her with this vision of setting up a school which provides comfort and stress-free environment for young children.

She is blessed with three handsome sons.
